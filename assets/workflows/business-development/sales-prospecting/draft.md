## Instructions
Produce a sales prospecting report with these sections:
### 1. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Refine the ICP for {{project_name}}'s current stage.
- Identify the key industries, company sizes, and job titles of the ideal users/buyers.
### 2. Qualified Leads (5-10 leads)
For each potential customer, provide:
- **Company Name & Website**
- **Rationale for Fit** — Why they need {{project_name}}.
- **Key Decision Maker/Persona** — Who at the company should be targeted.
- **Estimated Potential Value** — High / Medium / Low.
### 3. Sales Messaging
- Draft a value proposition tailored to each of the top 3 segments.
- Identify the most common objections and how to handle them.
### 4. Prospecting Channels
- Recommend the best ways to reach these leads (e.g., LinkedIn outreach, cold email, community engagement, events).
