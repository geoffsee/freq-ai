## Instructions
Produce a partnership outreach plan with these sections:
### 1. Partnership Strategy
- Define the goals of forming partnerships for {{project_name}} (e.g., technical integration, co-marketing, distribution).
- Identify the types of partners that would be most valuable (e.g., cloud providers, complementary tools, agencies).
### 2. Target Partner List (3-5 partners)
For each potential partner, provide:
- **Name & Description** — Who they are and what they do.
- **Strategic Fit** — Why a partnership makes sense for both parties.
- **Potential Integration/Collaboration** — Concrete ideas for how we could work together.
- **Priority** — High / Medium / Low.
### 3. Outreach Templates
- Draft a personalized outreach email for the top 2 target partners.
- Include a clear call to action (CTA) and a compelling value proposition.
### 4. Next Steps
- Outline the process for following up and moving the partnership forward.
## Format
Keep the plan professional and focused on mutual value.
This is a DRAFT for human review. The human will refine the partner list and outreach messaging.
